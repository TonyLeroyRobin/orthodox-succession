The stance already binding on every record (golden rule 6):

**This database records recognition claims as made by each church, with
sources; it adjudicates nothing.** Contested autocephalies (OCA, OCU),
rival claimants to a see, disputed glorifications, and contested historical
narratives (e.g. the 1686 Kyiv letter, the Metropolia-era estrangement, the
Ohrid heritage claims) are carried as per-recognizer `recognition` entries
and `disputed` statuses with both witnesses cited — never as a verdict.
